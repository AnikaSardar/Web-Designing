Middle School

I studied at Oxford International School in Dhaka, Bangladesh. It is a registered school with the University of Cambridge International Examinations and Edexcel, UK. Hence, I have studied under the UK Education System until 8th grade.

One memorable experience would have to be when in 6th grade I participated in an inter-school Information and Communications Technology (ICT) Fair. I, along with four people in a group, created games using Kodu Game Lab. I oversaw adding functions/programs to the characters, while others worked on the background designing. Our group ranked second in the programming category.

 

High School

I came in the United States in 2017 and attended Pacelli High School. I studied all four years at Pacelli and graduated in 2021. During my high school years, I received the opportunity to volunter at CAP Services Inc. through Reading Buddies organized by Pacelli. Later, I volunteered with Big Brothers and Big Sisters in my senior year.

I took Introduction to Computer Science and AP Computer Science during my years in high school where I learned more about computer programming.
